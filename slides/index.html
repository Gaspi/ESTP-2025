How to achieve this?

Fundamental principles

Open innovation


Open Innovation acts as a bridge between internal teams and outside networks

  • Share knowledge and resources
  • Wider pool of new ideas and technologies
  • Mitigates risks associated with new product development
  • Cost savings
  • Higher-quality products that better meet users needs

Fostering best practices

  • Autonomy
  • Separation of code, data and execution environment


Use cases

Use case: promote cooperation

  • A common infrastructure outside local IT
  • Access to standardized environments
  • Fit for work on open data
  • enables cross-INS projects

Use case: a project show room

  • Dissemination of new ideas and experimental work
  • Allows one-click test of advanced technologies / tools
  • Example: Lomas Promote open source

Use case: hands-on formations and tutorials


  • Reproducible environments
  • No configuration overhead
  • Trainees autonomy
